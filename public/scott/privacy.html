Privacy Policy

Scott's Mobile Dog Grooming LLC — Terms & Conditions / Privacy Policy. Scott's Mobile Dog Grooming respects your privacy. By opting in to our SMS messaging service, you agree to the following terms regarding how we handle your data:

Data Collection

We collect your name, email address, mailing address, and mobile phone number when you sign up for SMS updates. This information may be collected via our website contact form, appointment request form, or other booking system.

Data Usage

We use your data solely for sending updates, promotions, and reminders related to our grooming products or services.

Data Security

We protect your data with secure storage measures to prevent unauthorized access.

Data Retention

We retain your information as long as you are subscribed to our SMS service. You may request deletion at any time.

Message & Data Rates

Your mobile carrier may charge fees for sending or receiving text messages, especially without an unlimited texting or data plan.

Message Frequency

Messages are recurring; frequency varies, and you may receive up to 4 messages per month.

Opt-Out & Help

You can opt out of the SMS list at any time by texting STOP. After unsubscribing, you'll receive a final confirmation message and be removed within 24 hours. Text HELP for additional assistance, and you'll receive a reply including our phone number, email, and website link.

Non-Sharing Clause

We do not share your data with third parties for marketing purposes. Scott's Mobile Dog Grooming will not sell, rent, or share collected mobile numbers with anyone.

Contact
