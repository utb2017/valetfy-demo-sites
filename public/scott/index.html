Good to know

Frequently asked questions

How do I schedule? +
Schedule through any "Book Now" link on this site, or tap to text Scott and he'll send the info to get you set up.
Where do you park? +
At your home or apartment. Please have a large parking space with overhead clearance — the van is long and about 9 feet tall. Street parking is preferred where allowed; if using a driveway, please make sure there are no low-hanging branches.
What's your cancellation policy? +
A 48-hour cancellation policy applies. Cancel 48 hours or more in advance and your deposit is automatically returned. Less than 48 hours notice or a no-show results in loss of the deposit — late cancels are very hard for a small business to recover.
Is a deposit required? +
Yes — a 50% deposit and a signed service agreement are required to schedule, covering the cancellation policy. Cancel 48+ hours ahead and it's returned; if the groomer cancels for any reason, your deposit is returned.
What about fleas, ticks & parasites? +
It's the owner's responsibility to ensure the dog is free of fleas, ticks, and parasites before the appointment. If any are found during the groom, the groom is cancelled and the dog returned to the owner, resulting in loss of deposit.
Can you groom anxious or senior dogs? +
Absolutely. Cage-free, one-on-one grooming at home is far calmer than a busy salon — ideal for nervous and older dogs, with patient handling throughout.
